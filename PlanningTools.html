There are four categorizations of essential items required
 1.  Silver/Bronze/Steel Items - Based on individual capabilities, purchase in either silver, bronze or steel.
2.  Puja Items: Specialized puja items that can be purchased and stored for a long duration of time.
3.  Common Puja Items:  Specialized puja items that are used regularly.  They either need to be purchased or prepared at home.
4.  Perishable Puja Items: Puja Items that need to be purchased just before puja day.
