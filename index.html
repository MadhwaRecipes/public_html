Overview

Work Life Balance!  New generation women's biggest challenge!  Managing work, raising
kids,  inculcating our tradition and culture is something an NRI woman constantly working
towards.

With stressful and busy life, celebrating festivals seem like a daunting task and add
tremendous stress to already stressful life.  Our effort is to make festivals less stressful and
more fun and memorable for your family.

Raised in a Madhwa family and being abroad and a working wife for more than 20 years,  I feel
that I can provide some tips to make achieve that work life balance.

This site provides easy suggested recipes for each Madhwa festival that would  less than 2
hours and still complete all the formalities.

Our site is dedicated to all the new wives and moms﻿ who are trying so hard to make it in this
new challenging and wonderful world!!!!
